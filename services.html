OUR SERVICES

Virtual CFO Services

Our Virtual CFO services offer expert financial guidance to help your business thrive without the need for a full-time, in-house CFO. We provide strategic financial management, performance monitoring, and reporting to optimize business operations and ensure financial stability.

  • Financial forecasting and budgeting
  • Cash flow and working capital management
  • Financial performance analysis and reporting
  • Strategic planning and risk management
  • Compliance and regulatory oversight

Business Support Services

Our business support services provide administrative, financial, and operational assistance, enabling businesses to focus on core activities. From accounting to compliance, we ensure your business functions smoothly and efficiently.

  • Book keeping and accounting services
  • Payroll management and processing
  • Tax filing and compliance
  • Legal and regulatory compliance assistance
  • Fixed Asset and Inventory Management including Impairment analysis
  • Process consulting and advisory on ERP implementation and support
  • GL, Vendor and Customer Reconciliation services

Entity Restructuring services

We assist businesses in transferring ownership or restructuring their entities to achieve operational efficiency, tax optimization, and sustainable growth. Our experts guide you through legal, financial, and operational aspects for a seamless transition.

  • Structuring and restructuring business entities
  • Tax efficiency and regulatory compliance
  • Legal documentation and due diligence
  • Succession planning and ownership transfer
  • Post-transfer integration and support

Business Valuation Services

Accurately assessing your business’s worth is critical for decision-making. We provide comprehensive business valuation services, helping you understand the real value of your assets, liabilities, and market position for mergers, acquisitions, and investment purposes

  • Financial statement analysis
  • Market comparables and industry benchmarking
  • Discounted cash flow (DCF) analysis
  • Intangible asset valuation
  • Preparation of detailed valuation reports

Fund Raising

Our fundraising services are designed to help businesses secure capital through debt or equity financing. We offer strategic advice on the best funding options to support business expansion, working capital needs, or new projects.

  • Investor pitch preparation and presentation
  • Equity and debt structuring
  • Liaison with investors and financial institutions
  • Due diligence and compliance support
  • Capital raising strategy and negotiation
